How much experience do I need in yoga/meditation?
None.

What equipment do I need?
Have your yoga mat, towel and a bottle of water with you.

Do I need a specific outfit?
Wear flexible and comfortable sports clothes.

Can I do yoga if I'm pregnant?
Yes, we have qualified yoga teachers. It's advisable to begin yoga after the first trimester and with your doctor's permission.

Is there an age restriction?
Anybody over the age of 16 is welcome to our classes.

What is your cancellation policy?
Please contact us, and we will discuss it with you.
